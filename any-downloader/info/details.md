# Any Downloader

Download audio and video from YouTube and 1000+ other sites. A tray icon sits in the system tray — click it to open the download window.

## Supported formats

- **MP3** — 320 kbps or 128 kbps
- **WAV** — lossless audio
- **Original audio** — raw source format (WebM, M4A etc.)
- **Video** — all available resolutions from 360p up to 8K

## Supported sites

YouTube, Vimeo, SoundCloud, Twitter/X, Instagram, TikTok, and 1000+ more via yt-dlp.

## How it works

- Toggle **ON** — installs yt-dlp if missing, launches the downloader window
- Toggle **OFF** — closes the downloader window cleanly
- Paste any URL into the input bar and hit fetch
- Choose your format and click download
- Downloaded files go to your selected folder (default: Downloads)

## Requirements

No manual installs needed. The platform provides Python and yt-dlp is installed automatically on first enable.
